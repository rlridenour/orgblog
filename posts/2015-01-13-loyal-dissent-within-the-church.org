#+title: Loyal Dissent Within the Church

#+begin+quote
What if Christian colleges and universities --- even those affiliated with traditions with highly literalistic doctrines of creation --- embraced a picture of unity in the body of Christ that included the concept of necessary loyal dissent within a framework of basic respect, transparency, and honest searching for truth?
#+end_quote

--- Ronald E. Osborn, /Death Before the Fall: Biblical Literalism and the Problem of Animal Suffering/
